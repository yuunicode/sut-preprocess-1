<h2>4.1 생산 지수</h2>

<p>주) A : 실적출선량</p>

<p>※ 강풍감산량 기준</p>

<p>: 감산량 20% 이상 &amp; 강풍량 20% 이상시</p>

<math display="block">4.1.3 \text{ 가동율 (\%)} = \frac{\text{조업율} \times \text{작업율}}{100} = \frac{A}{A + B + C} \times 100</math><img alt="Equation-Block snippet" src="components/3d418eba161b9c21242b81e88d4a845e_18_img.webp"/>

<math display="block">4.2.8 \text{ 보정코크스비 (kg/t-p)} = \frac{100 - \text{분석수분 (\%)}}{100 - \text{보정수분 (\%)}} \times \text{코크스비 (kg/t-p)}</math><img alt="Equation-Block snippet" src="components/952215a8f9eddf9d2b9b486ee1582ccc_10_img.webp"/>
